mtable

Tests for MathML 4 mtable presentation attributes via mtable/mtable.js. Each row repeats the same MathML twice: the middle column is outside any polyfill wrapper (native mtable); the right column is wrapped in mtable-test-polyfill-root and processed with _MathTransforms.transform so you can compare native rendering with the polyfill. Unless a case is explicitly a 0×0 or 1×1 table, every mtable here has at least two non-empty rows or two non-empty columns.
